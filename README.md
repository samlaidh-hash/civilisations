A strategic civilization-building card game where 2-12 players compete to build the most cultured civilization across three historic ages.

## Overview

Lead one of six ancient civilizations (Rome, Greece, Egypt, Persia, Carthage, or the Celts) through three ages of development. Build your economy, raise armies, construct wonders, and outmaneuver your opponents through clever card play and strategic timing.

## Features

- **6 Civilizations**: Each with unique starting bonuses
- **3 Ages of Development**: Progress from basic resources to powerful wonders
- **Multiple Victory Paths**: Win through culture, military conquest, or economic dominance
- **Card Synergies**: Build combos with resource production, military units, and civic structures
- **Manual Abilities**: Activate special card powers at strategic moments
- **Reactive Triggers**: Cards that automatically respond to other cards being played!
- **War & Diplomacy**: Wage war to ruin opponents' cards or maintain peace
- **AI Opponents**: 3 difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard) - AI uses manual abilities!
- **Game History**: Track all actions with the 📜 History button
- **Beautiful UI**: Themed graphics with ancient civilization aesthetics

## Quick Start

### Installation

1. Ensure you have all game files in the same directory:
   - `CIV 5.html` - Main game file
   - `*.svg` / `*.jpg` - Icon images for resources, card types, and civilizations

2. Open `CIV 5.html` in a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)

### Setup

1. **Add Players**: Click "Add Player" to add 2-12 players
2. **Configure Each Player**:
   - Enter player name
   - Choose Human or AI (Easy/Medium/Hard)
   - Select civilization (each has different starting gold and bonuses)
3. **Start Game**: Click the green "Start Game" button

### First Turn Walkthrough

1. **Age I begins**: Each player receives 7 cards and starting gold
2. **Place Phase**:
   - Review your hand
   - Click a card to place it (costs gold)
   - Can't afford? Click "Pass" to gain 3 gold instead
   - All players place simultaneously
3. **Reveal Phase**: Cards are revealed and paid for automatically
4. **Activate Phase**: Card abilities trigger (gold, resources, military)
5. **Next Turn**: Cards pass clockwise, repeat for 6 turns
6. **End of Age**: Conquest (military leader gains culture) → War (strong attack weak)
7. **Age II & III**: New cards, untap all abilities, repeat cycle

## Game Concepts

### Resources

Resources reduce card costs by 1 gold each:
- **Animals** (A) - From herding and domestication
- **Food** (F) - Agriculture and sustenance
- **Knowledge** (K) - Schools and learning
- **Metal** (M) - Mining and smithing
- **Stone** (S) - Quarries and masonry
- **Valuables** (V) - Gems, gold, luxuries
- **Wood** (W) - Forestry and lumber

### Card Types

- **Economic** (e) - Generate gold and resources
- **Military** (i/m/r/n) - Infantry, Mounted, Ranged, Naval units
- **Civic** (c) - Government buildings and culture
- **Fortified** (f) - Defensive structures
- **Wonder** (w) - Magnificent achievements
- **Trade** (t) - Commercial ventures
- **Holy** (h) - Religious sites
- And more...

### Victory

**Most Culture Points wins!**
- Earn culture from card values
- Win military conquests (2/4/6 culture per age)
- Some cards provide culture bonuses
- Convert remaining gold (3 gold = 1 culture)

## Tips for New Players

1. **Start Economic**: Early resource cards pay off long-term
2. **Balance is Key**: Need both military (for conquest) and culture (to win)
3. **Pass Strategically**: Sometimes gaining 3 gold is better than a bad card
4. **Watch Opponents**: Track their resources and military strength
5. **Manual Abilities**: Look for the ⚡ button - use abilities at optimal times
6. **Reactive Cards Matter**: Spearmen counter Chariots, Chariots counter Spearmen!
7. **Fortify Early**: Fortified cards protect you from wars (including reactive Watchtower)
8. **Build Synergies**: Agoge + Infantry, or Arabian Horses + Mounted = powerful combos
9. **Age II/III Power**: Later ages have more powerful but expensive cards
10. **Check History**: Use 📜 History to review what triggered and when

## Controls

- **Click Card in Hand**: Place card (if you can afford it)
- **Pass Button**: Skip turn and gain 3 gold
- **⚡ Button on Cards**: Activate manual abilities (appears on certain cards in tableau)
- **Next Phase Button**: Advance to next game phase
- **Menu Button**: Access game menu, rules, or start new game
